How To Play

  • Click on something to look at it
  • Use WASD for cardinal movement and QEZC for diagonal movement
  • Walk into enemies to attack
  • Walk into chests and doors to interact
  • Use i to open your inventory, then the letter shown to use an item
  • Use m to open your magic
